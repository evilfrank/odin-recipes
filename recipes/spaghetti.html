Spaghetti

Description

Traditional spaghetti dish that is afforable and quick to make with a substution of ground beef or sausage with ground turkey to avoid red meats.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean ground turkey
  • 1 12oz package of spaghetti noodles of choice
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 small can of tomato paste
  • 1 can of dice tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of mice garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of italian seasoning
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 small package of slice white mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil of choice
  • 1 teaspoon of salt and ground pepper

Steps

  1. heat dutch oven or sauce pan to medium heat and add olive oil
  2. add ground turkey and let brown
  3. bring 4 quarts of water to a boil and add spaghetti noodles cook to preference
  4. once ground turkey is browned remove ground turkey and remove drippings from pan
  5. dice onion and cut mushroom to desired size and add pan with olive oil
  6. after veggies have cooked and tomato sauce, dice tomatoes and tomato paste
  7. readd ground turkey
  8. add minced garlic
  9. stir well
  10. add seasonings
  11. bring mixture to rolling boil reduce heat after boil to let simmer and stir occasionally
  12. let simmer for 45 minutes to hour
  13. server over spaghetti
  14. Enjoy!
