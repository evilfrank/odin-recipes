Red Beans and Rice

Description

Red beans and rice is a southern favorite dish, popular in Lousiana. The dish may traditionally is cooked with Ham Hocks however, I prefer to substitute that with andouille sausage. Although, you may switch the sasuage with a regular smoked beef sasuage for a more mild dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of andouille sausage.
  • 1 can of dark red
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 1 yellow or white onion your preference
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2 cups of chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon of your favorite salt free Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 2 cups of your favorite brown or white rice
  • 1 half stick of butter

Steps

  1. thinnly slice sausage
  2. heat pan to medium and add half stick of butter
  3. add sausage to pan and let brown during next step remember to stir frequently
  4. cut onion, celery and bell pepper into small pieces
  5. remove sausage from pan after browning
  6. add chopped veggies to pan that sausage browned in
  7. allow veggies to cook in pan with drippings till onion until soft
  8. drain can of beans then add be sure to mix well
  9. after beans mixed add chicken stock
  10. start cooking the 2 cups of rice with prefered method
  11. readd sausage to pot and bring up to a boil
  12. once at boil add all seasonings then reduce to simmer remember to mix
  13. after rice is down mash 3-4 spoonfuls of beans on side of put then stir
  14. serve over rice with prefered hot sauce!
  15. ENJOY!
