Chicken and Rice

Description

Chicken and rice is quick and easy dish to make for the family in about 30 minutes on a busy weekday that the family will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of boneless and skinless chicken thighs or breasts
  • 1 bag of mixed vegtables corn, green pepper, onion and carrot
  • 2 cups of your favorite white or brown rice I would recommend basmati
  • 1 half stick of butter
  • 1 cup of chicken stock
  • 1 teaspoon of sage and another of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of red cayenne pepper and another of red pepper flakes optional adds spiciness
  • 1 avacado ripe

Steps

  1. cube chicken breast or thighs with knife or kitchen shears
  2. heat a dutch oven or sauce pan to medium heat
  3. start cooking the 2 cups of rice of choice
  4. add cubed chicken and let brown
  5. add 1 cup of chicken stock once chicken is brown
  6. add mixed veggies and bring to light boil
  7. add seasonings
  8. cover once up to a boil and reduce heat to simmer
  9. be sure to stir often and not to over cook veggies
  10. slice avacado
  11. serve chicken and veggies over rice and top with sliced avacado
  12. Enjoy!
